Based on our industry experience, we help organizations make wise decisions that strengthen their business proposition and improve HSE performance.

At PVAN Canada Inc, we serve national and international clients, helping them make wise environmental and safety decisions that lead to improved organizational performance, and a stronger business proposition in the long term.

Our team of experts fully invest in understanding your business drivers. This commitment to truly knowing our clients allows us to deliver tailored results that consistently exceed expectations and foster high levels of satisfaction.

Our team brings a wealth of experience in Occupational Health and Safety, Environmental Safety, Accident/Incident Reporting and Investigation, Business Continuity and Emergency Management, Ergonomics, Construction Safety, Compliance, and Safety Management Systems.

We have highly qualified professionals, including a Certified Safety Professional (CSP) accredited by the Board of Certified Safety Professionals (BCSP) USA, who also holds an MBA in Total Quality Management (TQM).

In addition, our team includes a Canadian Registered Safety Professional (CRSP) and a Certified Lead Auditor for ISO 45001:2018 (Health and Safety Management Systems), ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management Systems), ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management Systems), ISO 27001:2013 (Information Security Management Systems), and ISO 22301:2019 (Business Continuity Management Systems).

Our experts, each of whom have over 20 years of experience, have successfully developed and implemented safety systems in industries such as Aviation, Nuclear Energy, Oil and Gas, Rail and Road Transport, Logistics, and Behavior-Based Safety. This enables us to deliver practical and effective solutions to complex safety and compliance challenges across various sectors.
