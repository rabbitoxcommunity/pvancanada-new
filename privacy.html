Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08 January 2025

Pvan Canada Inc (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. We may collect the following personal information from you:

This information is collected when you:

The personal information we collect is used for the following purposes:

3. How We Store Your Information

Your personal information is stored securely on our systems. We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us
